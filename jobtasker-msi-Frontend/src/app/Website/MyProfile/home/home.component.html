How to make your listings a success

  • Your response time determines your listing's ranking

    The time you take to respond to a customer’s booking request is a big factor that determines your listing’s ranking in search results. You should respond quickly to every request, whether you make an offer or politely decline. Our most responsive Taskers respond within 1 hour.

  • Never miss a booking request

    Once you receive a booking request, we will let you know by sending you a notification. Make sure you don’t miss out by enabling ‘Task updates’ in your notification settings and downloading our free app. You can find all booking requests in My Tasks.

  • Optimize your listings to gain more exposure

    High quality listings are ranked first. Check out our tips and tricks on how to improve your listing, boost your ranking and increase your chances of being booked!
